About me

Hi! Thank you for taking the time to view my portfolio. My name is Noah Yuen and I am a third-year software engineering student at Cal Poly SLO. My first couple years of undergraduate studies were spent as an industrial engineering major, where I completed almost all of my engineering support coursework as well as a handful of industrial engineering and software engineering major courses. I changed majors to SE during winter quarter of my second year. Since completing my first programming course during Fall of 2019, I have fallen in love with the analytical and visual aspects of software engineering and the delightful solutions that I am able to create through my laptop. I am very detail-oriented and enjoy expressing my creative side, which is why I spent this past summer delving into the world of web development and design. This coming school year, I hope to develop my programming skills to the point where I can seriously contend for a software engineering/web development position for the Summer of 2021.

In my free time, I enjoy shooting and editing creative videos with my drone (Mavic Air 2) and GoPro (Hero 8) using Adobe Premiere Pro. In the future, I hope to combine my skills in both software and film to create delightful content. I also enjoy strategic boardgames, travel, intense workout sessions and spending time in the great outdoors.
